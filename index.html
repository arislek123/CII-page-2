CII Dashboard — Attained / Required / Rating

Calculate Attained CII from annual fuel consumption and distance. Required CII is computed from reference line (a,c) and reduction factor Z. The dashboard includes rating bands (A–E), CO₂ breakdown by fuel, grade sensitivity vs distance, fuel-switch optimizer, and annual tightening projection.

Quick tips: Use annual distance and annual fuel on the same time basis. Distance affects the denominator (capacity·nm), while fuel affects emissions. Use Correction factor = 1.00 unless you intentionally apply adjustments.
